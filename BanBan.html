Who is BanBan?

Banban, known as Case 6, was one of the first mascots created at Banban's Kindergarten. Initially given a human-like circulatory system, the experiment failed, leading to a switch to a Givanium-based system. Upon coming to life, he retained memories of his human genome donor, Dr. Uthman Adam, believing himself to be the experimenter. Despite not needing to eat or drink, he does so and, like other cases, experiences unexplained constant pain. Highly intelligent and fluent in speech, he remains docile and cooperative but struggles with the reality of his lab-created existence.
