Who is Bitter Giggle

Bittergiggle is an energetic and playful jester whose greatest joy comes from making others laugh with his jokes. His purpose is to spread happiness, and when he is able to do so, he thrives. However, when left in isolation or unable to fulfill his role, he begins to lose his grip on sanity, becoming erratic and over-the-top in his attempts to entertain.


Despite his wild energy, Bittergiggle has moments of surprising calm and thoughtfulness. He is capable of forming meaningful bonds with those around him, showing a deeper, more emotional side beneath his jester persona. While his primary goal is to bring joy, his desperation to do so can sometimes lead him down a darker path.
